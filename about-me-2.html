About Me

 

I'm a Senior at Purdue University pursuing dual degrees in Computer Engineering and Math with a minor in Psychology. I'm interested how we do computing. For too long we've thought of computers as machines that execute line after line of code. This runs counter to the spatial and nonlinear way that our universe works. Can we change our hardware, software, and interactions with machines to overcome the bottleneck of sequential processing? 

When I'm not contemplating the how to revolutionize computing, you can find me playing guitar, running, or reading.
